Singlethread Video Inspections

Why video?

Don't worry. It's easy.

Videos help you sell and create a better experience for customers. With Singlethread's easy-to-use technology, video is easy to implement. It's as easy as 1, 2, 3.

How It Works

1.

Shoot it


A technician simply opens the Singlethread mobile app on a tablet or mobile device, select a component that needs repair, and captures a video in a matter of seconds.

Video Best Practices
2.

Review it


Sometimes, mistakes can happen. That's why we have an advisor or manager review videos prior to submission to a customer.

3.

Send it


Videos are displayed directly in Singlethread's Quick Quoting tool. Advisors simply add a price to the repair and click send. It's as easy as that. In an instant a customer has a professional quote and video on his or her phone delivered by Singlethread's reliable text messaging.
