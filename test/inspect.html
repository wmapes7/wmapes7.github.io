Vehicle Inspections

How Singlethread is different.

Vehicle Inspections for the Mobile Era

Singlethread Inspect is vehicle inspections for the mobile-device era. Singlethread Inspect is fast and light while supporting a team-based approach. It eliminates the grease-stained paper forms of days-past and creates mobile inspections, quotes, and customer-approvals that result in more CP work.

Mobile-First

Singlethread Inspect is built for dealership mobile devices and mobile customers. Use tablets or desktops and easily communicate with mobile customers so you sell more.

Fast

Time is money, that's why "fast" is critical to Singlethread. Techs complete inspections in minutes and Advisors quote just as quickly to deliver inspection results to customers in an instant.

Team-based Approach

Techs, parts, advisors - all working together, quickly and efficiently. Role-based permissions and views make it easy to complete your part and move on with your day.

Customized

Do you have a custom inspection form you'd like integrated? Singlethread can customize your forms for mobile devices. Contact us to see how.

Reduces Tech Frustration

Techs get frustrated when they inspect vehicles and advisors don't review reports with customers. Singlethread inspections get reliably delivered.

Seamless Integration

You're always seconds away from your inspection results. Just tab into Inspect and you're on your way. It only takes seconds.

Endless Partnership Opportunities

The rich data that is generated and captured in Inspect offers countless opportunities for OEMs, dealers, or 3rd parties. With real-time data export, partnering with Singlethread and it's dealers can generate leads, follow-up activities, shape marketing plans - the opportunities are endless.

OEM Business Intelligence

Capture a real-time data stream from your dealers directly to your systems. Contact us and see how Singlethread can help you execute on your opportunities.

Digital and Traditional Marketing
