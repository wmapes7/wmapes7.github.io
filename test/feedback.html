Solve your follow-up problem

Singlethread automatically follows-up with customers for you. Completed in real-time and on-schedule, you'll never have to worry about the follow-up process again. With built in notifications and dashboards, you'll spend less time following-up and more time on generating revenue.

Singlethread can be configured to automatically follow up for you. Using a manual template can also add a nice personalized touch.
