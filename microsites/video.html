Singlethread Video

Singlethread Video is the easiest way to share video from your service bays directly to your customers. Singlethread video is included with Inspection at no additional charge.

HOW IT WORKS

1

Download or Login

Simply login to www.singlethread.com or download the app on your mobile device. The Singlethread mobile app is available on the Apple App Store and the Google Play Store.

2

Inspect

Start an inspection on your mobile device. Select a component in yellow or red and tap the video button.

3

Record

Record the vehicle component that needs repair.

4

Review & Price

On the quote page, advisors or managers review the video and add a price.

5

Send

The customer recieves the video and a quote on their mobile device. With a simple tap, they'll see the video and aprove your quote.
