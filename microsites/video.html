How It Works

1. Shoot


A technician simply opens the Singlethread mobile app on a tablet or mobile device, select a component that needs repair, and captures a video in a matter of seconds.

2. Quote


Quickly review videos and add a quote in Singlethread's quoting tool. Mistakes can happen, that's why Advisors or Managers review the video prior to submission to the customer.

3. Send


Customers recieve a video and quote all in one easy to view mobile-format.
